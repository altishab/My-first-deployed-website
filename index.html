The Biography

My name is Altin Shabani. I am currently enrolled in Georgian College for Computer Tech. I live in Bradford and I have lived here since I was eight years old. There are four people in my family. Two brothers and my mother and father.

I was born in Richmond hill. I had lived there for about six years up until I had moved to Brampton. When I was eight years old I had moved to Bradford which I have lived here until now. I have attended plenty of schools. The first school I attended was Richmond hill public school. Up until grade four. I moved to brampton which I attended a public school there. Until I was eight and moved to Bradford I then attended Fieldcrest Public school. Then I attended CHPS short for Chris Hadfield Public school. After that I attended Bradford district highschool. After that I am now attending Georgian College. I've made a few achievements. Getting a Job. Graduating Highschool. Attending College Building a computer. Learning how to snowboard. As of now I have a few goals. My first goal is to actually graduate Georgian with flying grades. After that my next goal is to make as much money as I can and retire young.
